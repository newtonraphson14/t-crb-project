# AAVSO T CrB Campaign Context (optional)

Main campaign source:
- Alert Notice 750 page: `https://www.aavso.org/aavso-alert-notice-750`

Campaign dates found:
- Active dates: `Aug. 3, 2021 - Sept. 30, 2021` (as listed on Alert Notice 750 page)

Monitoring objective (short summary):
- AAVSO requested coordinated photometry and spectroscopy monitoring of T CrB to support scheduled HST and XMM-Newton observations in Aug-Sep 2021.
- The stated goal was to track accretion and wind-mass-loss behavior prior to the expected next nova eruption and provide context for the space-based observations.
